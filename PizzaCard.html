Veg Pizza

Begin your cooking journey with this wholesome veg pizza recipe, crafted to deliver a balance of melted cheese, garden-fresh toppings, and irresistible aroma straight from the oven

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Capsicum
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Olives
  • Oregano & chili flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C.
  2. Spread pizza sauce on the base.
  3. Add cheese evenly.
  4. Add chopped vegetables.
  5. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes.
  6. Bake for 12–15 minutes.
  7. Slice and serve hot.

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Serving Size: 2 people

Timer: 30:00
